Actor.setCrimeBoss()

USE: 
Make this person the boss of your crime family.

TYPE: Command, Non-Ref
RETURNS: /
THEME: Crime
COMPARE: addCriminal
EXAMPLE:
    Boss.setCrimeBoss()
    Boss.dress()
    Boss.show(2)
    Boss(Evil):: "So, you want to join our Family? Have you actually thought this through or are you just another wasting my time?"
