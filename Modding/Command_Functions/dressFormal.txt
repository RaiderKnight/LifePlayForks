dressFormal()

USE:
Use this to toggle the 'dress code' of the scene.

TYPE: Command, Non-Ref
RETURNS: /
THEME: Clothes
COMPARE: dressWedding(), dress()

EXAMPLE:
    dressFormal()
    Actor.dress()
    Actor2.dress()
    dressFormal()
    Actor3.dress()
    
    "1 and 2 are wearing suits while 3 is looking like a bum"
