Actor.dressWedding()

USE:
This command puts the actor in a wedding dress or suit.

TYPE: Command, Reference
RETURNS: /
THEME: Relationships
COMPARE: getMarried, dress

EXAMPLE:
	"It took quite a bit of doing, getting into that corset, but I finally was good to go, and ready to step down the aisle."
	setBackground(place_of_worship)
	Player.dressWedding()
